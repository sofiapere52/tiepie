Preview

Put the pointer over a plot and scroll the wheel to zoom the time axis (page scrolling and Ctrl+page zoom are blocked there). Drag to pan; double-click to reset. The preview never uses a higher sampling rate than the hardware will for these settings. All three plots stay in sync. After you zoom in, wait a second or two for the plot to update: the app refetches that time window at the real hardware sample rate so you see the genuine waveform, not a coarse overview.
